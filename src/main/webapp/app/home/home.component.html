Features
  • Create a grocery list
  • Manage purchased ingredients
  • Create the recipes and share them with others
  • Find a recipe that you can make from your purchased ingredients
Shared recipes

On this page are all public recipes that you can copy (1.) into your list or assign all ingredients to the shopping list.

Recipes

This space allows you to manage and add your favorite recipes. Search recipe by name or find recommendations (1.). Recommendations are based on the ingredients you purchased.

Shopping list

In the grocery list are two lists - with ingredients that you want to buy and purchased ingredients. Available actions:

  1. edit an ingredient
  2. remove one or all from the shopping list
  3. move ingredients to the purchased list
  4. remove one or all from the purchased list
  5. move ingredients to the shopping list
  6. move ingredients to the bought list
Ingredients

It's your virtual fridge. To remove an ingredient click (2.) or (1.) for all of them, (3.) and (4.) moves all or one ingredient to the shopping list accordingly.
